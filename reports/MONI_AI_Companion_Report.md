# MONI 3.0 AI Companion Experience Specification

## 1. Executive Summary
MONI has transitioned from a standard command workspace into an intelligent, highly personalized AI Companion. This specification outlines the visual orb updates, contextual suggest pathways, and local data routing guidelines.

## 2. Living Visualizer Orb States
The central Moni Orb now breathes and reacts to live status changes:
* **Idle:** Smooth breathing scaling effects.
* **Listening:** Glowing turquoise contrast adjustments.
* **Thinking:** Continuous rotation with yellow accents.
* **Speaking:** Alternate scaling.
* **Working:** Amber gradient pulses representing active background workflows.
* **Warning:** Critical alerts flashing red.
* **Happy:** Soothing green gradients when memory updates succeed.
