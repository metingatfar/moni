# MONI 3.0 Daily Brief and Productivity Hub

## 1. Structure
The Daily Brief acts as the user's start-of-day morning card, highlighting:
* **Task Load:** Counter showing total uncompleted todos.
* **Memory Count:** Registered facts and preferences in SQLite.
* **System Connectivity:** Active voice outputs and database connectivity checks.

## 2. Continue Working State
Caches last active directories, chats, and modulator adjustments to provide instant resume links.
